Movement

Use the WASD keys on your keyboard for movement. If you are on a mobile device, they will be provided for you.


Aiming and Guns

Use the A and D keys to move the iron sights of your hunting weapon left and right. Once you have your target in sight, press the X key to fire. If you are on a mobile device, the keys will be provided for you.


Survival/Basic Game

Chop wood, hunt animals, and pick berries and trap fish to survive. Protect yourself from raiders, federal officers, and ally yourself with tribes, townsfolk, etc.
